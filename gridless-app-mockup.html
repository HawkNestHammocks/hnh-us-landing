📊 YOUR READINESS SCORE
How it works
One number for how ready your household really is — right now.
0–19
JUST STARTING
20–59
BUILDING
60–100
READY
⬆️ It goes UP when you act:
· Daily missions (+1 to +3 each)
· Program days (+2) and finished programs (+5)
· Family challenges — everyone's score rises together
⬇️ It DRIFTS DOWN when you don't:
· Skip a full week → score slips
· Batteries age, seasons change, plans go stale — readiness isn't a trophy, it's a state. The score tells the truth.
🏆 At 100: you're fully ready — for now. Keeping it green takes a few minutes a day. That's the whole game.
