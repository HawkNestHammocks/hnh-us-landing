Frequently Asked Questions

What makes this a V.3?
The V.3 is the third generation of the Hawk Nest, featuring improved waterproof fabric, stronger spreader bars, a redesigned entry system for easier access, upgraded stitching throughout, and a more compact packed size. Every major pain point from V.1 and V.2 has been addressed.
How hard is it to get in and out?
Very easy! The V.3 features a redesigned wide entry that lets you get in and out smoothly. Most people compare it to getting into a regular bed. No awkward climbing or balancing required.
How stable is the Hawk Nest?
Extremely stable. The spreader bar system creates a flat, taut sleeping surface that doesn't sway or flip. You can toss and turn, sleep on your side, back, or stomach without any instability. It supports up to 500lbs.
What is the weight capacity?
The Hawk Nest supports up to 500lbs. The heavy-duty straps, reinforced stitching, and aircraft-grade aluminum spreader bars are designed to handle serious weight without compromise.
What's the maximum height it can accommodate?
The Hawk Nest comfortably fits people up to 6'6" tall. The roomy interior gives you plenty of space to stretch out fully without feeling cramped.
Can more than one person use it?
The Hawk Nest is designed as a single-person shelter for maximum comfort. While it's spacious for one, we recommend one Hawk Nest per person for the best experience. Many couples and families bring two!
