Our Story

Built By Hawk Nest.
For Our Community.

We started Hawk Nest Hammocks because we believe the outdoors should be accessible, comfortable, and safe for everyone. After helping thousands of campers sleep better in the wilderness, we asked: what else can we do?

That's how Gridless was born. We realized that while our hammock gets you out there, knowledge is what keeps you safe. Whether it's purifying water, building a shelter, navigating by the stars, or knowing what to do when the power goes out — these are skills everyone should have.

So we built the app we wish we'd had growing up. No internet required. No fluff. Just real survival knowledge wrapped in a game you'll actually want to play.
